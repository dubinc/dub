You can now invite teammates to your Dub project. This is useful if you want to collaborate with your team on a project.

Dub has the most generous free plan in the industry. You can invite up to 2 teammates to your project (3 total users) for free.

Need to invite more teammates? Upgrade to our [Pro plan](/pricing) for unlimited teammates.

Learn more about [inviting teammates](/help/article/how-to-invite-teammates).
